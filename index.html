What are Frankenswitches?

The idea of a ‘frankenswitch’ refers to the cobbling together of various parts from different switches to create something new. Now the world of frankenswitching is fraught with strife as a lot of combinations do not work, with housings refusing to close, switches refusing to actuate and other issues that may occur. In this world of failed experiments there are however, some gems that have been etched into the consciousness of enthusiasts in the hobby.

Here are some of the recipes of several frankenswitches that may interest you!
